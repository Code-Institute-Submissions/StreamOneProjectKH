Rumours of a ps4 bundle

23 Oct 2017

Recently, it has been rumoured that a new collection will be making its way to the PS4. Despite already having the collections (1.5 and 2.5) with the recent addition of 2.8 to that roster, everything is already available for anyone who would like to catch up with the story.

But who could resist the cash grab of bringing all three of these games together to tide people over until the eventual release in (supposedly) 2018!

Kairi EX and redefining the meta

16 Oct 2017

Kingdom Hearts Unchained X, a simple game with so many complexities that for some it just becomes a hardcore grind with a power creep that makes some players unable to even experience some of the small interspersed story peppercoated into this near 700 (currently for global) level medalfest.

Coming soon to a phone near you is the massively coveted Kairi EX, a medal that according to a large part of the Kingdom Hearts subreddit is changing the entire meta. No setup or build will be without this medal, making the rest of the content available much more manageable for everyone!

Character details confirmed!

9 Oct 2017

Some of the most important news we've had so far. What we have always wanted to know is the heights of our favourite characters. Nomura confirmed that the height of Sora is a meager 5ft2! Crazy how short he is considering his big ol' feet!

This has led to research being done online to find out the heights of other characters by using the available character models through data mining. Although this can be slightly weird since it would make characters like Lexaeus over 7ft tall. These games aren't exactly known for their realism so you never know, these could be the actual heights!

Another exciting piece of news we've been told is Sora's birthday. Much like many game characters, the main characters birthday tends to be the debut of the first game they are apart of. This means that Sora's birthday is 28th March. Not the most game breaking of news nor does it really add to the overall plot but it is something!
